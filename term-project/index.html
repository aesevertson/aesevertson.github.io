Many temples we are located by have either great shopping places, unique historical sites or breathtaking coastlines that can be visited as well.

The Gilbert Temple was announced in April of 2008 by President Thomas S. Monson. The groundbreaking for the temple began in November of 2010. The dedication took place on March 2, 2014. The Gilbert temple was dedicated by President Thomas S. Monson.

The Laie Temple was announced in November of 1915 and the groundbreaking for the temple occurred in February of 1916. The first dedication took place in 1919 being done by President Heber J. Grant. It was rededicated twice since then; in 1978 by President Spencer W. Kimball and in 2010 by President Thomas S. Monson.
