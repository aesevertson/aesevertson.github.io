Alignment

href="https://www.spotify.com/us/" target="_blank">spotify.com

The design principle of alignment is when different elements of a page are aligned in relation to each other an another part of the page. This can occur both vertically and horizontally. The design principle of alignment is an important element to consider in web design because it helps the content of a page to look neat and consistent making it easier to navigate and read. A website that uses the design principle of alignment well is the Spotify mobile browsing site.

Visual Heirarchy

href="https://www.shutterfly.com/" target="_blank">shutterfly.com

The design principle of visual heirarchy is defined as the order in which the human eye perceives what one is vewing. This design principle is an important part of a good web design because it helps to draw the user's attention to the most important element on the page and so on. A good example of visual heirarchy used in mobile browsing is with the site, Shutterfly.
