Site Purpose

The purpose for this site is to provide visitors to the page information about the latest weather condition for a selection of towns in the Southeast Idaho area of the country. These towns include Preston, Soda Springs and Fish Haven. The home page on the site will feature a weather story along with an image and information on each of the three towns. Each town will have a page that provides current weather conditions, images of the town and its current weather condition, and any other current and relevant information related to each town.
