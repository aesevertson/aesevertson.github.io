Target Audience

The target audience for this site are people who want to stay updated on the weather. This can be for people who already live in the area or are looking to visit. The typical site vistors are college aged students and older. Most site visitors come to see what the weather is and if it is ideal for the outdoor activities that can be done in Southeast Idaho.
